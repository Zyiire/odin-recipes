Chicken Parmesan

This is a delicious recipe for Chicken Parmesan.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. In a bowl, mix breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Dredge each chicken breast in the breadcrumb mixture.
  5. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  6. Fry the chicken breasts until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Place the fried chicken in a baking dish.
  8. Spoon marinara sauce over each piece of chicken.
  9. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  11. Serve hot with pasta or a side salad.
