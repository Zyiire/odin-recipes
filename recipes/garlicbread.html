Garlic Bread

This is a simple and delicious recipe for Garlic Bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of French bread
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and salt until well combined.
  3. Slice the French bread in half lengthwise.
  4. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over both halves of the bread.
  5. If desired, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Place the bread halves on a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before slicing into pieces.
  8. Serve warm as a side dish or appetizer.
